Who are we?

A Whisk Through Time: Our Rich History and Timeless Reputation

We are Crepe'Licious, we take pride in whisking you away on a delectablee journey through time with our mouth watering crepes.
Established with love and passion 10 years ago, we have been an integral part of our community's culinary landscape, delighting countless taste buds and creating cherished memories for our cherished customers.

Our Journey to Flavorful Creations:

Our story began with a dream, a dream to create a warm and inviting space where people from all walks of life could savor the taste of France through the simple joy of crepes.
From the very first day we flipped a crepe on the griddle, we knew that our mission was to craft these delicate delights with an unwavering commitment to quality, taste and innovation.

An Array of Choices, A World of Flavors:

We believe that the true beauty of crepes lies in their versatility. Whether you're a sweet tooth aficionado or a savory enthusiast, our menu boasts an impressive array of choices that will leave you spoilt for options. From classic favorites like Nutella and strawberries to savory delights like ham and cheese, our crepes cater to every palate and craving.

Our Commitment to You:

At the heart of everything we do is our commitment to our valued customers. Your satisfaction is our topmost priority, and we strive to ensure that each visit to Our Shop is a delightful experience. Our friendly and attentive staff are always ready to welcome you with a smile, eager to assist you in selecting the perfect crepe that suits your mood.

We are more than just a crepe shop; we are a place of gathering, laughter, and cherished moments shared over delicious crepes. Join us at CL and become a part of our ever-growing family.
Come, step into our world of flavors, and let us whisk you through time, one delightful crepe at a time.

Our Secret Ingredient

Guess what sets us apart ?

Every crepe that leaves our kitchen is infused with a secret ingredient ♥ love ♥. From the finest ingredients to the skilled hands that craft them, each crepe is made with passion and a sprinkle of magic.
